Carbon Footprint Data Sash

An exploration of personal ecological data


This project aims to help individuals better understand their own carbon footprint through the creation and donning of a personalized data sash.

By wearing the sash in public, it also acts as an intermediary object to spread public awareness, adding to the discourse of understanding personal and systematic environmental impact.
