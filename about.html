Hello!

I'm Stephanie Dykes, and I love to make things. I am a passionate and detail-oriented UX designer who believes in designing products for the customer's needs.

I am a recent graduate from Georgia Tech's M.S. Digital Media program and am based in Atlanta. I have lived here since 2012, when I started my undergraduate degree at Georgia Tech. Through my courses and internships with ADP and The Home Depot, I've learned the value of putting the customer's needs first and ensuring that design decisions are made with the end user in mind. I value input from stakeholders and users in the design process, and I love to facilitate collaboration so that everyone's voice can be heard.

I believe in remaining humble in the design process and confronting every problem with a curious and open mind. In my spare time I enjoy pottery, sewing, hobby electronics, dance, and hosting events. I love bringing thoughtful design into anything that I work on, whether it is a product or an experience.

If you have any questions or are interested in freelance work, feel free to contact me below!
