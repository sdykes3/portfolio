Lantern - Data Analytics Dashboard

June - Aug 2017

The Problem

Contact center data was plentiful, but gathering and analyzing it was time and labor-intensive. Senior managers did not know about issues until long after they occur and are reported up the chain, making them late to reallocate resources.

The Solution

Lantern enables quick discovery and analysis of issues. It renders contact center data as both raw accessible transcripts and trending issues, dynamically categorized via machine learning.

Team:
